FAQs

Do you have a question? Cause we got an answer.

  • What is your goal? Our goal is giving you the possibility to share anything you want using just you voice.
  • Is it free-to-use? Yes, it's free, always and forever.
  • What do you mean with "radius of appearance of an audio"? When you create an advert you must indicate a radius of appearance of it, it's the area in what the ad will be visible from the user, that means that an user will listened it if the distance between the user and the ad is lesser than the radius of appearance.
  • What does happen with the audio when it's deleted? It's removed from our server.
  • Is my data secured? We've got some experts in security protecting all the data generated in SoundGo.
  • Where are your servers located? In the United States of America.
